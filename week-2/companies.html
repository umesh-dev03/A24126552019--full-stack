About the training and placements

Founded by NRI philanthropist Dr. N.B.R. Prasad in loving memory of his late son, Anil Neerukonoda, the institution is situated on a sprawling 12-acre campus. ANITS is permanently affiliated with Andhra University (AU) and has steadily grown into a hub of academic innovation, modern infrastructure, and research-driven learning. Through robust support from the Training and Placement Department, students frequently secure top-tier placements, with the highest package reaching Rs 12 lakhs. The college also operates as a recognized Business Incubator under the MSME Incubation Scheme, fostering entrepreneurship and technical excellence.

Department Focus

  • Identifying employment opportunities for fresh engineering graduates.
  • Building strong industry-academic partnerships.
  • Providing a comprehensive campus recruitment training program.
  • Organizing industry expert lectures, workshops, and interactive sessions.
  • Promoting innovation and entrepreneurship development.
  • Facilitating internships, industrial visits, and technical collaborations.
